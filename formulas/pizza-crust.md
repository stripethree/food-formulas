# Pizza Crust

### Components

* 3 c flour
* 2 teaspoons (1 packet) instant yeast
* 1 tablespoon olive oil
* 2 teaspoons kosher salt
* 1 teaspoon garlic powder
* 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
* 1 1/3 cups warm water
* Cornmeal

### Assembly
- Pre heat the pizza stones!
