# Apple Compote

### Components

* 10 apples, peeled and diced
* 3 tbs butter
* 1/3 c brown sugar
* 2 1/2 ts cinnamon
* 1/2 ts allspice
* 1/2 ts clove
* 1/2 ts nutmeg

### Assembly
* Melt butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat
* Add brown sugar and stir until dissolved
* Add remaining ingredients
* Simmer over medium heat until apples are tender
