# Maraschino Cherries

### Components

* 1/2 cup white granulated sugar
* 1 ts vanilla
* 1 cinnamon stick
* Pinch of nutmeg
* 1 tbs lemon juice
* 1/2 cup water
* 1 pound cherries, stems and pits removed
* 1 cup Luxardo maraschino liqueur

### Assembly
* Combine sugar, vanilla, cinnamon stick, nutmeg, lemon juice, and water in a medium saucepan.
* Bring the mixture to a simmer, cover, and allow to cook for 5 minutes
* Reduce heat to low and add the cherries. Cook the cherries at a simmer for about 3 minutes, until theyâ€™re slightly softened
* Remove from the heat and stir in the Luxardo liqueur
* Allow the mixture to cool completely.


### Notes
[Original recipe](http://www.acozykitchen.com/homemade-maraschino-cherries/)

I store these in a mason jar after they have cooled. I remove the cinnamon stick when storing them as I have found it continues to break down and the cinnamon flavor strengthens.
