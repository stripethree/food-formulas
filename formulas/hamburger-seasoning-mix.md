# Hamburger Seasoning Mix

### Components

* 2 tsp black pepper
* 2 tbs brown sugar
* 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
* 2 tsp cumin
* 2 tsp garlic powder
* 1 tbs onion powder
* 2 tbs paprika
* 2 tsp salt

### Usage
* Use 1 tbs for each pound of ground beef for hamburgers
