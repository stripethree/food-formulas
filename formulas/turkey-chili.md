* 2 cans (30 oz, 3-3/4 c) diced tomatoes
* 1 tbs chili powder
* 1 tsp ground cumin
* 1 tsp pink Himalayan salt

* 1/2 tsp onion powder
* 1/2 tsp garlic powder

* combine all ingredients into a crock pot
* cook on low for 6 hours
